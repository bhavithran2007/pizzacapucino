Our Story

Every flavour Tells a Story

Capucino, founded by Ilayaraja in Trichy in 2008, has enjoyed a successful 16-year journey, expanding to three branches in Kattur, Thillai Nagar, and Chathiram Bus Stand. Dedicated to offering delicious, artificial-free food, Capucino has earned a loyal customer base. Their focus on quality ingredients and flavourful pizzas has driven their growth and popularity. Ilayaraja's vision of serving natural, satisfying meals resonates with patrons seeking authentic dining experiences. Capucino continues to thrive, providing a taste of excellence in each dish, connecting communities through the joy of good food.
